Pickled salmon with Noilly Prat

Pickling salmon yourself is very easy. Pickling is one of the oldest methods of preserving fish.
13 April 2023
Khadija El Azzouzi

As an Amazon Associate, I earn from qualifying purchases.

Ingredients

  1. 400 g salmon fillet, with skin
  2. 50 g sugar
  4. 50 g salt
  5. 100 ml Noilly Prat
  6. 1 small bunch of dill
  7. 5 g juniper
  8. 5 g fennel
  9. 5 g pepper
  10. 5 g anise
  11. 1 lemon zest or orange zest
  12. Wasabi Mayonnaise
  13. Herb Sponge Sponge Cake (in microwave)

Ingredients

Put all the spices in the mortar or coffee grinder, and it is possible not to grind them very finely
For the pickling mixture: we need chopped dill, zest or zest of lemons or oranges, sugar, salt and ground spices.
Wash the salmon fillet and pat dry.
Rub with Mozilla Part.Spread the mixture evenly over the salmon fillet
Cover the fish with a layer of cling film. Weigh down with a cane or jam jar. Chill in the fridge for about 8 hours.
Unwrap the salmon fillet, rinse under cold water, pat dry and remove from the skin, brush with Noilly Prat and flambé briefly.
To serve, cut the salmon into thin slices and serve with the herb sponge cake and wasabi mayonnaise.
Optionally, you can additionally decorate with salmon caviar: 500 g Gorbuscha - Salmon Caviar PREMIUM 100 g Keta - salmon caviar
